University of Washington MSGT

The School of Urban Studies at the University of Washington, Tacoma, offers a Master of Science in Geospatial Technologies degree. The degree provides advanced training in GIS, instructing students on operation and application of geospatial hardware, software, and data in urban and environmental planning scenarios. This program prepares students to become leaders in the management and utilization of geospatial technologies within the public, private, and not-for-profit sectors. It also offers training in the development and deployment of location-based mobile applications and management of web-based geospatial data. This program maintains a theoretical/critical focus on the application of these technologies to urban and environmental problem solving.
