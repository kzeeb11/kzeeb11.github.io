Health Equity in Pierce County, WA

A closer look at the health of residents in the South puget Sound

Leaflet map using geojson data for Michigan bridge ratings. The information was obtained from Michigan Department of Transportation, and includes data on all the bridges in the state. I put the shapefile in ARC, selected bridges by safety rating and saved the individual layers. I used QGIS to convert the new shapefiles into geojson and added them to my Leaflet map. I used the cluster plug-in for the bridge points, and linked the pop-up to bridge location. TI am woring on creating a toggle legend for good, fair, or poor bridge rating.
