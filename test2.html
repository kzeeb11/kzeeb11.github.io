The (Interactive) Global Sharknado Threat

Lifted directly from John Nelson using his styling, I present an interactive web-based version of his instant classic The Global Sharknado Threat map

In John's words, "This is The Global Sharknado Threat Map. Created by an unprecedentedly cross-discipline team of nameless chain-smoking scientists in white lab coats, this map is intended as a resource to coastal communities regarding the nature and likelihood of a sharknado incursion. Right?"

In my words - this map is amazing. I'm using the data on his tutorial page from noaa hurricanes since 1891, catagories 3, 4 and 5 plus Great White and Hammerhead shark populations and habitat data.
