University of Washington MSGT

The Urban Studies Program at UW Tacoma offers a Master of Science in Geospatial Technologies degree. The degree will provide advanced training in GIS, training students to use and apply geospatial hardware, software, and data in urban and environmental planning scenarios. It prepares students to become leaders in the management and utilization of geospatial technologies within the job market -- public, private, and not-for-profit sectors. It also offers training in the development and deployment of location-based mobile applications and management of web-based geospatial data. This program maintains a theoretical/critical focus on the application of these technologies to urban and environmental problem solving.
