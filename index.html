Graphic Design is my passion and I let my work speak for me.

In the past two years I have received the following recognitions: Winner (3rd place Large Format Printed Maps) at the ESRIUC 2017 Map Gallery; A series of my maps were published in the 2018 ESRI Map Book #33 and 2019 Map Book #34. I am a recent recipient of the HOW Regional Design Award, a prestigous design industry recognition. And recently paced first at the 2018 WAURISA conference map gallery for the Women in Stem map project (designed for the Washington Women in Geospatial Technology organization). I am currently a 2019 masters candidate at University of Washington for Geopatial Technologies.



Graphic Arts /Design
